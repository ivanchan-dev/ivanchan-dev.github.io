The Background

Why Renovation Bundling?

Currently, Serviced Office (SO) bundles capture baseline operational costs, but custom renovation investments are frequently tracked in siloed systems, leading to delayed billings and tracking disconnects.

By integrating Renovations natively into the SO workflow, we achieve centralized cost control, structured handoffs to Capital Development, and unified downstream contract records.

Leakage Risk

Renovations omitted in initial deal summaries fail to route to downstream contract lines, reducing profitability tracking accuracy.

Compliance Gaps

Deals closing without verified contractor quotations cause operational bottlenecks during project executions.

Fragmented Systems

Manual re-entry of renovation figures into monthly contract databases increases transactional overhead.

Optimized Solution

A unified, automated, validated pipeline ensuring structured tracking from day one of the deal setup.

Compliance Engine

Data Validation & Restrictions

To maintain strict corporate oversight, two mandatory business rules protect downstream operations from entering dirty or unapproved deal data:

Mandatory File Upload

Triggered upon attempting to progress Opportunity Stage to Closed Won with any Renovation Item bundled. Requires a document specifically named Renovation Quotation.

Line Item Cap Limit

Limits users to exactly one (1) Renovation Product record per Opportunity to prevent overlapping scopes of work on a single contract.
